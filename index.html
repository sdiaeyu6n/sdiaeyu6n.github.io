About

I am an M.S./Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Dongguk University, advised by Prof. Jihie Kim. My research focuses on multimodal learning and interactive AI, especially at the intersection of vision-language modeling, sketch-based reasoning, and human-robot interaction.

I previously worked as a Visiting Research Scholar at Carnegie Mellon University (Bot Intelligence Group), supervised by Prof. Jean Oh (Aug 2025 to Feb 2026). I also conducted research at Dongguk University's AI Lab and completed a visiting scholar program at Purdue University (M2M Lab).

For Ph.D. study, I am interested in building reliable multimodal systems that can reason about human intent, perception, and context in real-world interaction settings.
