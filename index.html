Diffusion Model Enhancement for Sketch Generation via VQA Feedback
Sieun Choi
IJCAI, 2025 (Submitted)   /   Patent (in progress)
project page / paper

We adapt Stable Diffusion to instance-level human-drawn sketches and introduce a VQA-based reward to improve prompt fidelity and semantic alignment. Includes a new dataset of instance-level sketches with captions and QA pairs.
