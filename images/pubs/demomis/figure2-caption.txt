Overview of our controlled audit protocol. We construct a benchmark from FairFace source portraits and pair each portrait with diagnostic edit prompts designed to stress-test identity preservation. For each image–prompt pair, we evaluate three open-weight I2I editors under standardized inference. Outputs are scored by a VLM ensemble and validated on a sampled subset through human annotation. For the mitigation case study, an observable feature prompt pfeat is prepended to the original edit instruction.
