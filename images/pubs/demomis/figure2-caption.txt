Overview of our study on demographic-conditioned failures in instruction-guided I2I portrait editing. We build a controlled
benchmark from FairFace and pair source portraits with edit prompts. For each image–prompt pair, we run three I2I editing models to
generate outputs. For diagnosing soft erasure and stereotype replacement, we evaluate iedit; for feature prompt mitigation, we add a feature prompt pfeat and re-run editing. Outputs are assessed via human evaluation and a VLM ensemble.
