SketchDUO construction process. The pipeline proceeds from left to right. It begins with class selection and sketch definition, followed by positive sketch collection and negative sketch construction. The two subsets are then augmented. In the caption generation stage, GPT-4o produces template-constrained instance-level captions for positive and negative sketches, and these captions are manually refined before TIFA-based QA pairs are constructed from each sketch--caption pair. The final SketchDUO dataset contains 35,851 sketch--caption pairs and 54,370 QA pairs.
