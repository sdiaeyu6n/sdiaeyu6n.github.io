Overview of the proposed boundary-aware class-balanced replay mechanism. Source-task samples are evaluated as
replay candidates using class-presence and boundary-complexity statistics, which are combined into a priority score for replay-buffer construction. High-priority samples are inserted or retained through priority-based buffer updates, and replay mini-batches are sampled according to priority-proportional probabilities during target-task training. Red, green, and blue regions denote the left ventricular endocardium, left ventricular epicardium, and left atrium, respectively, while yellow contours indicate extracted boundary maps.
